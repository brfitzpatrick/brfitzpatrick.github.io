Homepage of Ben R Fitzpatrick

I'm a postdoc at The Swiss Federal Institute for Forest, Snow and Landscape Research. I'm investigating natural and anthropogenic influences on the distributions of red wood ants in Swiss forests. In 2017 I completed my PhD and graduated in December. Prior to that I studied Zoology and Marine Biology and completed an honours project at the Centre for Marine Futures in Western Australia.
