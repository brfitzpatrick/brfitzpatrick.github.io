Welcome to the homepage of Ben R Fitzpatrick

Hello, I'm a spatial data scientist/quantitative ecologist. I've worked as a postdoc at The Swiss Federal Institute for Forest, Snow and Landscape Research where I investigated the influences of red wood ant distributions in Swiss forests. Prior to this I completed my PhD on statistical methods for soil mapping and prior to that I completed an honours project on species distribution modelling of demersal fishes.
